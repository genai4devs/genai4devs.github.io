What is Generative AI?

AI (Artificial Intelligence) is the intelligence of machines or software, as opposed to the intelligence of human beings or animals (Wikipedia)

Generative AI is AI capable of generating text, images, or other media, using generative models (Wikipedia)

Generative AI is not new: it was introduced in the 1960s in chatbots, e.g.: ELIZA created by J. Weizenbaum

What are the major breakthroughs since then?

  • 2000s, development of Neural Networks thanks to Big Data and GPU
  • 2014, Ian Goodfellow: Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)
  • 2017, Google: Transformer Architecture
  • which led to Large Language Models (LLMs)
